Conclusions

What the data tells us

Not a seller problem

Matt converts 37% of intros to demos — top of all AEs. His actual selling is strong. The gap is in how he logs deals in HubSpot.

Not an ICP problem (mostly)

Glyphic data shows primary stall reasons are budget constraints, competitor lock-in (Quorum), and timing — not ICP mismatch. Only 2 of 14+ stalled deals were truly bad fit.

A deal definition problem

Deals are being created before buyer commitment exists and then held back because AEs aren't confident they're real. The process doesn't match how buyers actually move.

The fix: Automate early stage movement. Demo scheduled → deal auto-created. Demo completed → auto-advance to Discovery. This removes the behavioral variable, gives consistent data regardless of AE confidence level, and keeps pipeline measurement at Discovery+ meaningful. Matt still exercises judgment on Active Negotiation onward — we just take the early stages out of his hands.
